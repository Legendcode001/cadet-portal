Core Values

  • Integrity: Upholding the highest standards of honesty and ethics.
  • Discipline: Maintaining self-control and following the established code of conduct.
  • Commitment: Demonstrating unwavering dedication to the mission and goals.
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest quality in all endeavors.

History of Cadet Corps in Nigeria

The Nigerian Cadet Corps program has been pivotal in shaping the country's future leaders. Established to instill discipline and leadership qualities among the youth, it plays a crucial role in national development. Cadets undergo rigorous training, participate in community service, and learn essential military skills, preparing them for various future roles.

Benefits of Joining

  • Leadership training that builds confidence.
  • Physical fitness programs to enhance health.
  • Opportunities for scholarships and higher education.
  • Networking with professionals and community leaders.

Training Programs

Our training programs include physical fitness, leadership development, survival skills, and teamwork exercises. Cadets also participate in community service projects, helping to build character and a sense of responsibility.
