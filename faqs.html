What is the purpose of the 3rd Brigade Headquarters Cadet Program?

The purpose of the 3rd Brigade Headquarters Cadet Program is to develop future leaders through structured training, instilling values of discipline, teamwork, and responsibility in young individuals.

How can I apply to join the Cadet Program?

You can apply by visiting our application page and filling out the online form. Make sure to provide all required documents and follow the application guidelines.

What are the eligibility requirements for joining?

Candidates must be between the ages of 12 and 20, possess a good academic record, and have a strong commitment to the program's values and mission.

What kind of training will I receive?

Cadets will receive comprehensive training in leadership skills, physical fitness, military tactics, and community service, preparing them for various roles in the Army and beyond.

Are there any costs associated with joining?

Yes, there is a one-time registration fee, which is used to cover training materials and uniforms. Additional costs may apply for special training programs.

Can I participate in the program while attending school?

Absolutely! The program is designed to accommodate academic schedules, and we encourage participants to maintain their studies while engaging in cadet training.

What benefits do cadets receive from the program?

Cadets gain valuable leadership experience, develop physical fitness, receive mentorship from military personnel, and have opportunities for scholarships and advanced education.

How can I contact the program for more information?

You can reach out to us via our contact page, or you may call our office directly at the phone number listed on the page.
